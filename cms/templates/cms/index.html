About

Jiu Bar is a high-end Asian Fusion restaurant and bar that prides itself on serving exceptional Asian cuisine using innovative Western cooking techniques. Located in the heart of downtown Calgary, the restaurant is helmed by renowned chef Pei Yu.

The restaurant's unique concept was developed by one of Calgary's most popular designers, Dylan Luc. He was inspired by a vision of Jiu Bar in his dream and brought it to life through his branding concept.
